Who We Are

Our Story

Founded in 2018, WWT Music Club was born from a shared love of live music and the belief that great sounds bring people together. We welcome musicians of all skill levels, from beginners picking up an instrument for the first time to seasoned performers with decades on stage.

Our community hosts weekly jam sessions, workshops, open mic nights, and major seasonal concerts. Whether you play guitar, keys, drums, or sing — there's a place for you here.
